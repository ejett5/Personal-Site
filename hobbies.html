Woodworking

In a contrast to working with modern technology slowing down and enjoying traditional woodworking is a great way to unwind while making something for my friends and family. There is also a soothing rhythm to everything when working the wood that is beautiful. Future Projects are always good and give me something to look forward to. Anything I do for my family is worthwhile, and more rewarding when I see the smiles on their faces.

There is also a satisfaction of doing a task that requires concentrated effort that is different from daily tasks and different way of thinking with the immediate reward of seeing how the action comes into reality.
